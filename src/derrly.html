What is Derrly AI

You hire an AI game studio.
Through conversation.

Not a chatbot. Not a generator. An autonomous AI game studio — a team of 12 specialized agents that collaborates to build complete games from your ideas.

01 — The Problem
Game development takes years and millions of dollars.
A typical indie game requires engineers, designers, writers, and artists working for 1–3 years. Most ideas never become games because the barrier is insurmountable.
$250k+ cost 1–3 years Large team required High failure rate
02 — The Solution
Derrly replaces the entire team with AI agents.
Describe what you want. An Executive Producer dispatches 11 specialists. They collaborate, argue, revise, test, and deliver a complete playable game — in minutes, not years.
Free to start Under 60 seconds No team needed Automated QA

Creative Director, World Architect, Quest Designer, NPC Designer, Gameplay Engineer, Economy Designer, Balance Specialist, Multiplayer Engineer, UI/UX Designer, QA Tester, Game Builder — plus an Executive Producer orchestrating them all.
